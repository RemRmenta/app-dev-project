VR & AR Website Development Project


We are a group of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) students from Sorsogon State University - Bulan Campus, undertaking a project to customize and enhance a pre-existing BootstrapMade template. This project aims to develop an interactive and informative website centered around Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR). Through this endeavor, we will refine our skills in web development, content presentation, and deployment strategies.
